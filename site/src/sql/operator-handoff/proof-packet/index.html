unknownIdentity, ordering, parsing, or coverage does not support a stronger status.

Stops, gaps, and owners

The packet ends at the point where operator judgment begins.

  • Stop at every manual context transition until the active client tab, connection, server, database, schema, and role are independently verified.
  • Stop at protected-material boundaries and route handling through the approved owner process.
  • Stop on missing, conflicting, unknown, or owner-review permission evidence until a DBA validates the prerequisite candidate.
  • Keep dblink, logical-subscription, archive-link direction, and reduced-coverage gaps visible.
  • Ask who owns execution order, scheduled-operation review, independent validation, and cleanup review outside TraceMap.

Public claim boundary

This packet supports review questions, not operator authorization.

  • It does not execute SQL, connect to a live database, or observe an active database client.
  • It does not establish setup completion, effective permissions, object existence, replication health, scheduled execution, validation results, or successful rollback.
  • It contains no raw SQL, credentials, connection strings, scheduled command bodies, local paths, private infrastructure names, raw database output, or internal ticket details.
  • It does not replace DBA/operator approval, security review, change control, tests, telemetry, logs, or human judgment.
  • The core scanner and reducer use no LLM calls, embeddings, vector databases, or prompt-based classification.
