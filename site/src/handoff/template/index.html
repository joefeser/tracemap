Evidence handoff template

Keep one static-evidence handoff attached to its proof boundary.

Use this concept-level template when a TraceMap evidence question moves from one reviewer or owner role to another. It keeps the claim, proof path, deterministic evidence fields, limitation, non-claim, validation evidence, next owner, and stop condition in one public-safe structure.

Public claim level: concept. No public conclusion without evidence.

Use the template Proof paths Limitations Validation

When to use it

Use it for one bounded claim, question, or proof path.

The template is for transferring one static-evidence question to a human reviewer, owner role, or follow-up process. It is useful after a public-safe packet ingredient has been selected and before anyone repeats the conclusion. If the evidence is missing, private only, reduced, syntax-only, or still unvalidated, the handoff keeps that limit visible instead of upgrading the claim.

Template

Attach every field before a handoff leaves the review context.

Missing private-only values stay as explicit limitations or stop conditions; they are not silently omitted.
Field label Public-safe handling
handoff questionThe bounded question or claim being transferred, not a broad investigation topic.
audienceThe receiving role or review context. It is the receiver, while owner to ask is the role for the next open question.
proof pathA public-safe route, summary, rule catalog note, report-family summary, or private review location described only by function.
public claim levelUse concept for this template unless a cited public-safe evidence surface says otherwise.
rule ID/familyName the deterministic rule basis when public-safe; otherwise use a rule family and record the limitation.
evidence tierUse Tier1Semantic, Tier2Structural, Tier3SyntaxOrTextual, or Tier4Unknown.
coverage labelCopy the cited label or mark concept-only, demo-only, partial, reduced, gap, unknown, or syntax-only.
public-safe path/spanUse checked-in demo paths or sanitized references only; do not publish source excerpts.
commit SHAName public-safe scan context only. Missing or private-only values remain a limitation.
extractor versionName public-safe extractor context only. Missing or private-only values remain a limitation.
limitationState what the static evidence cannot prove.
non-claimState the stronger runtime, release, safety, ownership, completeness, AI, LLM, or review-replacement claim not being made.
validation evidenceName a public-safe validation result, command family, review evidence, or test summary without output or private logs.
owner to askName a role such as code owner, service owner, database owner, telemetry owner, test owner, release owner, or security reviewer.
stop conditionName why the handoff must pause, downgrade, remain private, or move to another owner.

Stop conditions

Pause the public handoff when a required boundary is missing.

  • missing proof path
  • private-only support
  • raw or private material
  • unknown or reduced coverage without label
  • unsupported runtime proof wording
  • unsupported release or safety wording
  • unsupported complete-coverage wording
  • AI or LLM analysis wording
  • no validation evidence
  • no owner to ask
  • blame language toward owners, reviewers, teams, vendors, or prior authors

Non-claims

The template does not prove, approve, automate, or replace review.

  • TraceMap does not generate this handoff, prove runtime behavior, production traffic, endpoint performance, outage cause, release approval, release safety, operational safety, or complete coverage.
  • TraceMap does not perform AI impact analysis, LLM analysis, autonomous review, or replacement of human review.
  • The template does not claim real organization ownership and does not assign work to a named person.
  • It does not replace source review, ownership decisions, telemetry, logs, traces, APM, tests, release controls, incident response, service-owner judgment, database-owner judgment, security review, compliance review, manager judgment, or human judgment.
